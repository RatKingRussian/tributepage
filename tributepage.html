The Demigod of the Golden Order

Facts about the great Radahn

  • Radahn is the son of Radagon and Queen Renalla. He is the epitome of Radagon's fiery red edemeanor and idolized the Great Elden Lord Godfrey
  • Radahn is ranked as one of the strongest Demigods among his siblings. He has conanoically defeated Melania who, in a desperate bid to not be seen as a little bitch, nuked the whole of Caelid with Scarlet Rot making it an empty wasteland and still being unable to kill the warrior.
  • In the early stages of the game of Elden Ring Radahn was such an intensely difficult fight FromSoftware was forced to nerf his hitboxes and damage in patch 1.03. (although the damage was later reinstated in a later patch)
  • Many people know of Radahn's great steed Leonard but less know that according to the Longtail Cat Talisman, Radahn had a beloved cat as well.
  • Radahn is the only Shardbearer in the game with no intro dialogue (this is seen as sensible as he has lost his mind.)
